OVERVIEW
WE HAVE SPECIALIST TEAMS

We know that medicine’s not limited to one discipline. That’s why we have specialist teams - Each trained for a specific field in the industry.

OUR PROCESSES WORK

You get the best returns possible and enjoy a healthy, consistent cash flow - All thanks to developed best practices in our operations, management, accounting and systems environments.

WE CROSS THE T’s AND DOT THE I’s

Unlike our ‘Double Entry System,’ many of our competitors only have one source of info to draw up monthly statements.

Our algorithms match up medical fund info with relevant bank statements, and automatically update your administrative system, daily.

USER-FRIENDLY TECHNOLOGY

Enjoy transparent access to all practice records with our innovative technology from Goodx Software and Dotmed Solutions.

FEATURES

SUBMITTING OF CLAIMS

We submit claims to medical funds on your behalf - updating and advising you of the outcomes.

FOLLOW UP SERVICE

We are in constant communication with your patients and their medical funds. This allows us to settle claims quickly, which automatically increases your cash flow. All conversations are recorded and discussion-related notes are digitised for immediate viewing on the Goodx System

INVOICING

We generate and submit invoices to patients (where applicable) as soon as claims are due. Invoices are always in your preferred format.

FINANCIAL BRIDGING

Our financial bridging keeps your revenue cycle healthy through pre-payment of invoices before settlement.

OPS SUPPORT

Our ops support team is always just a call or an email away. So don’t ever worry about a lack in support services - We’re ready & equipped to solve just about any problem

PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT

Your portfolio is managed by Senior Executives who keep a watchful eye on your business. They take note of trends and developments, and are always on stand by to discuss the progress of your portfolio.

ONLINE REPORTING

View management reports, notes, reconciliations, debtors ageing, payments and receipts, and much more right here on our website - 24 hours a day. Specific reports can be requested at any time should you need a more detailed analysis.

SITE VISITS

We love to come visit you at your place of work. It is here where we explain the workings of management reports and how to deal with any hiccups that your practice may be experiencing.

DEBTOR PAYMENT PORTAL

The payment portal on our website accepts all major credit cards and EFT, making it quick and easy for your patients to settle their accounts.

GOODX FRONT END

We are partnered with Goodx Software for the most cutting-edge technology in Practice Management Software. Data is seamlessly shared between your office and ours, and safely backed up on the Goodx servers.
